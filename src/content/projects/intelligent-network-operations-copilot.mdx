## Overview

This starter project entry shows how to position a technically serious AI system without slipping into vague product language. The framing is intentionally operational: network telemetry, investigation workflows, and deployment discipline.

## Problem

Operations teams often have the data they need, but not in a form that is fast to query, easy to interpret, or consistent across shifts. The aim of this project is to show how AI can support analysis without pretending to replace domain expertise.

## Approach

- Combined structured telemetry, historical incidents, and knowledge-base material into a retrieval-backed workflow.
- Designed outputs as operator aids: summaries, anomaly context, escalation prompts, and next-step recommendations.
- Kept the system explainable through source grounding and deterministic guardrails around actions.

## Why it matters

The strongest AI work in infrastructure environments is rarely about novelty alone. It is about reliability, traceability, and whether the system helps skilled people move faster without lowering trust.
